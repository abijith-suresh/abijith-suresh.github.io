A full-featured music streaming platform with playlist management, personalized recommendations, and offline playback capabilities.

## Features

- **Music Library**: Browse and search millions of tracks
- **Playlists**: Create and share custom playlists
- **Recommendations**: AI-powered music suggestions
- **Offline Mode**: Download tracks for offline listening
- **Social**: Follow friends and share music

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React, Redux
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express
- **Database**: PostgreSQL
- **Storage**: AWS S3 for audio files
- **CDN**: CloudFront for global delivery

## Technical Challenges

Implemented adaptive bitrate streaming and efficient caching strategies to provide smooth playback across varying network conditions.
